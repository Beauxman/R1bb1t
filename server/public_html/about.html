About us

Mission Brief

R1bb1t.com is a social media application developed by Andrew Bowman to demonstrate how modern tools and frameworks can be used to make effective and useful web applications without the need for extremely exhaustive budgets or development teams.

By providing users the ability to create, share, and connect with one another, R1bb1t achieves many of the core paradigms necessary to making a modern social media platform.

A Social Media Network

Post, Share, Like, Follow

An essential part of every social media platform is the ability for users and content creators to express themselves and their thoughts in a easy and intuitive way. Technology is supposed to make this process as simple and understandable as possible for the end-user.

Programmers are the ones that bridge the gap between complicated technologies and everyday people looking to post about their day.

Technologies Used

Languages:
- HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL

Front-end frameworks and libraries:
- Boostrap V3, AJAX, JQuery, Moment.js

Back-end frameworks and libraries:
- NodeJS, MySQL, Apache

Lessons Learned

Making this application was a very educational experience. R1bb1t is heavily inspired by Twitter and implementing some of the features of that platform required research and problem solving.

The process taught that knowledge of long-standing technologies is important, but also, being able to quickly learn and integrate new technologies on the fly is vital to programming.
